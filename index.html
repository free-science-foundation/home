Free Software, Free Society

Scientific tools for everyone, everywhere

This platform focuses on free and open source scientific tools that help bridge the gap between well-funded companies and underbudget university groups, independent researchers, and developing nation startups with great ideas but limited access to capital and resources.

Our Mission

We curate a comprehensive list of scientific tools from the community, focusing on free and open source solutions that balance inequalities in research access. Our mission is to empower underbudget university groups, independent researchers, and developing nation startups with the same powerful tools available to well-funded companies, ensuring great ideas can flourish regardless of financial resources.
