Meet Our Team

Passionate About Free Science

Our team is composed of dedicated researchers, developers, educators, and advocates who believe in the power of free and open source scientific tools. We come from diverse backgrounds but share a common mission: to make scientific research accessible to everyone, regardless of funding or resources.

Meet the Team

Our Values

We are driven by a commitment to scientific excellence, open collaboration, and educational empowerment. Our team members bring expertise from various scientific disciplines, software development, and community organizing to create a comprehensive approach to democratizing scientific tools.

Core Team Members

Our core team brings together expertise from computational science, software development, education, and community building. Each member contributes their unique skills to advance our mission of making scientific tools freely accessible.

Research Scientists

Experienced researchers who understand the practical challenges of scientific work and the need for accessible tools.

  • Expertise: Various scientific domains (biology, physics, chemistry, data science)
  • Focus: Tool evaluation, research workflow optimization, training development
  • Background: Academic and industry research experience
  • Contribution: Ensuring tools meet real research needs

Software Developers

Open source contributors who build, maintain, and improve the scientific tools in our ecosystem.

  • Expertise: Python, R, JavaScript, scientific computing libraries
  • Focus: Tool development, integration, performance optimization
  • Background: Open source project maintainers and contributors
  • Contribution: Technical implementation and support

Education Specialists

Educators and trainers who design and deliver effective learning experiences for scientific tools.

  • Expertise: Curriculum design, educational technology, pedagogy
  • Focus: Training programs, workshop development, documentation
  • Background: Academic teaching and corporate training experience
  • Contribution: Making complex tools accessible to learners

Community Organizers

Advocates who build and nurture our global community of users and contributors.

  • Expertise: Community management, outreach, partnership development
  • Focus: Community engagement, event organization, partnership building
  • Background: Open source community leadership experience
  • Contribution: Growing and supporting our user community

Community Contributors

Beyond our core team, we are supported by a vibrant community of volunteers, contributors, and partner organizations who share our vision. These include researchers who test tools, developers who contribute code, educators who provide feedback, and institutions that help us reach underserved communities.

Join Our Team

We are always looking for passionate individuals who want to contribute to our mission. Whether you're a researcher, developer, educator, or community organizer, there are many ways to get involved with the Free Science Foundation.

Volunteer Opportunities

Contribute your skills and time to help us expand our impact.

  • Content Creation: Write tutorials, documentation, and guides
  • Community Support: Help users in our forums and Discord
  • Tool Testing: Evaluate and review scientific tools
  • Outreach: Promote our mission at conferences and events

Partnership Opportunities

Collaborate with us to expand access to scientific tools.

  • Institutional Partners: Universities and research organizations
  • Technology Partners: Open source projects and companies
  • Funding Partners: Foundations supporting open science
  • Media Partners: Journals and science communication platforms
